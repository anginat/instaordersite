Is instaorder an app?

There is no app download or any sign-up required for guests to order/view your menu. instaorder is a web-app & works on any browser.

Do I get access to my customer data?

You own the customer data. You can access your customer data along with insights, sales performance, and various reports via your business dashboard.

How much commission does instaorder charge?

ZERO! Yes, we are a zero commissions platform.

Can I customize my menu?

You have complete control over your menu. Change item availability, descriptions & more from your business dashboard.

Can I upload pictures to my menu?

Yes, you can upload specific item pictures to your menu

Can my prices be different than my regular menu?

All prices are as per your discretion. We provide a business dashboard where you can change your item prices.

Can I use my own delivery fleet?

Thrive is your own ordering platform, you use your own delivery fleet to deliver your orders.

How does my customers access my menu?

For table ordering, customers can simply scan your QR code available at the table. You can download table wise QR codes from the dashboard. This opens up your menu on their smartphone. Customers do not require any app.

For delivery or takeaways, a unique URL is created that can be used to market to your customers via your channels. We advise you put it up on your social channels/whatsapp/sms, websites and aggressively market it using your sales channels.

Is their different status that I can assign to each order?

Each order can be assigned a status such as Accepted/Rejected, Ready, Completed etc. This helps you manage your operations seamlessly.

Update your Order App URL across all social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, Snapchat, Google etc.

Add “Order Now” button to your Instagram & Facebook.

Introduce discount coupons to attract as well as retain customers and generate repeat orders.

Add recommended food items to your Menu for your new customers.

How does my customers pay?

All credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI methods are accepted through your own payment gateway. All transactions are secure, and routed through Razorpay. All transactions will be credited to your RazorPay account which are usually settled in your bank account in 2-3 days.

How and when do I receive payments into my bank account?

Payment gateway takes care of all the bank settlements. All transactions will be settled to your bank account within 2-3 working days by the payment gateway provider. All payment gateways are RBI approved so you don't need to worry about payment settlements.

What are the payment gateway charges?

Payment gateway charges a transaction fee of 2% + gst on each transaction. For more details you may refer their website.

Can I set the delivery charges as per my requirement?

Yes. You can choose to add a fixed service/delivery charge (eg. Rs.30) in the Settings.
